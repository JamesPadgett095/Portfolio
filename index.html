Illustrations.

Album Cover Re-Make.
The Madcap Laughs, By Syd Barrett.

This album cover is one of my favourites from the Hipgnosis design group, However I could never find a high quality image of it.

I remastered the original cover into a high resolution illustration.

Here you can see the stages in which I created the seperate layers of the cover.

I then added the textures, these were either single colours or gradients.

The last step was editing the colours and effects to resemble the original better.

I wanted the cover to have a similar style to Hiroshi Nagai, as I am a fan of his work and took inspiration.

Sovereign Brand Graphic 5.

This design was originally inspired from the 80s airbrush art , in particular Hajime Sorayama. I wanted to go for that chrome futuristic look that had been so popular. I created gradient textures and had different sides have gradients facing differet ways to give the illusion of reflection. I then heavily edited the design.

I also came up with some fire and ice inspired versions whilst I was at it.

Brand Imaging:
Mindbend.

I decided to create a brand image inspired from the post-punk album covers of the late 70's and 80's. I also took inspiration from the architecture and stained glass windows of the Canterbury Cathedral.

Creation of Type.

I used simple shapes, such as rectangles, triangles and half-moons to create the type.

Design 1.

I have included a few different versions of the design. One to show the skeleton, another to show the first design idea, and the other to show a silhouette.

Design 2.

Here I have used textures to give the design an electric and messy look. I think this makes it more interesting and works well with the post-punk aesthetic I was going for initially.

Design 3.

I played around with distorting the shape of the design in this version. I wanted the design of the type to have meaning, so the logo describes itself.

Stained Glass Design.

I changed the colour and transparency of each shape I used to create the original design, to appear as if to be stained glass, much like many of the windows in the Canterbury Cathedral.

T-Shirts Design.
