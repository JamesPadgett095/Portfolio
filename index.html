Typography.

Typeface 1.

This is my first typeface. I used only simple geometric shapes to create it, as it was inspired by the Memphis Design Group.

I applied two textures, one for the border and one for the fill.

This is the finished product, with both textures applied.

Typeface 2.

This is my second typeface. For this one I wanted all the characters to fit into the same dimension.

For this I designed it to fit like a digital clock.

I created an illustration to demonstrate the idea of having type all the same size.

Illustrations.

Album Cover Re-Make.
The Madcap Laughs, By Syd Barrett.

This album cover is one of my favourites from the Hipgnosis design group, However I could never find a high quality image of it.

I remastered the original cover into a high resolution illustration.

Here you can see the stages in which I created the seperate layers of the cover.

I then added the textures, these were either single colours or gradients.

The last step was editing the colours and effects to resemble the original better.

I wanted the cover to have a similar style to Hiroshi Nagai, as I am a fan of his work and took inspiration.
